- **Focus**: Comprehensive guidance for entrepreneurship from 0 to 1
- **Course Rating**: 90.4/100 from students
- **Topics Covered**:
  - Entrepreneurial basics
  - Market demand identification
  - User research
  - Product construction and iteration (MVP development)
  - Business strategy
  - Team management
  - Finding financing
  - Investor connections

**Impact**:

- Many students continued building their projects after course completion
- Strong participation from freshmen, indicating early entrepreneurial engagement
